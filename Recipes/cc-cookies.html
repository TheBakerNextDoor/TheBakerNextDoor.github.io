Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • Unsalted Butter - 1 cup (softened) [2 sticks] - Phase 1
  • Light Brown Sugar - 1.25 cups (packed) [272g] - Phase 2
  • Granulated Sugar - 0.5 cup [100g] - Phase 2
  • Large Eggs - 2 (room temperature) - Phase 2
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tablespoon - Phase 2
  • Flour - 3 cups (loosely packed) [360g~390g] - Phase 3
  • Corn Starch - 2 teaspoons - Phase 3
  • Kosher Salt - 1.5 teaspoons - Phase 3
  • Baking Soda - 1 teaspoon - Phase 3
  • Chocolate Chips - 2-3 cups [208g]- Phase 4
  • Parchment Paper - 3 sheets (1 per pan) - Phase 5

Equipment

  • Cookie Sheet
  • Oven
  • Hand Mixer
  • Scraper
  • Large Bowl (Dry)
  • Large Bowl (Wet)
  • Scoop (Large / 3tb)
  • Fridge
  • Cooling Rack

Instructions

  1. Stage Ingredients
  2. With First mixing bowl (Wet)
    1. > , Add: Unsalted Buter (1c)
    2. Hand Mixer, Beat on Low: until light and fluffy (1min)
    3. > , Add: light brown sugar (1.25 cups)
    4. > , Add: granulated sugar (0.5 cup)
    5. Hand Mixer, Beat on Low: until creamed and smooth (3-5min)
    6. Use Scapper, on sides, as needed:
    7. > , Add one at a time: large eggs (2) (20sec ea)
    8. Hand Mixer, Beat on Low: (1 min per)
    9. > , Add: Vanilla Extract (1 TBSN) (20sec)
    10. Use Scapper, on sides, as needed:
  3. With Second medium bowl (dry)
    1. > , Add: Flour (3 cups)
    2. > , Add: baking soda (1 tspn)
    3. > , Add: corn starch (2 tspn)
    4. > , Add: kosher salt (1.5 tspn)
    5. > , Whisk: well to combine
  4. With Wet bowl
    1. From Dry Bowl, Add slowly: to wet
    2. Hand Mixer, Mix until combined: do not overmix
    3. > , Add: morsals (bag (2-3 cups))
    4. Use Scapper, fold ingredients:
  5. With Cookie sheets
    1. > , Add: parchment paper (3 sheets)
    2. Scoop, large: from mix, six per sheet
  6. With Fridge
    1. Fridge, chill: min 10 minutes per sheet and go directly into oven.
    2. Or Can chill over night, and remove from fridge 10min before baking.
  7. With Oven
    1. Oven, Preheat: 350
    2. Oven, Bake for: 12 min or until edges are golden brown
    3. Repeat, For each container: (after 10m dwell)
    4. Remove from tray to Cooling Rack: Keep on parchment paper
    5. Rest until cool
