Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Unsalted butter (softened) — 2 sticks
  • 1.25 cups Light brown sugar (packed) — 272 g
  • 1/2 cup Granulated sugar — 100 g
  • 2 Large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla extract
  • 3 cups All-purpose flour (≈390 g)
  • 2 tsp Corn starch
  • 1.5 tsp Kosher salt
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 2–3 cups Chocolate chips (≈208 g)
  • 3 sheets Parchment paper (one per pan)

Equipment

  • Cookie sheet(s)
  • Oven
  • Hand mixer (or stand mixer)
  • Scraper / spatula
  • Large mixing bowls (wet & dry)
  • Large scoop (≈3 tbsp)
  • Refrigerator (optional for chilling)
  • Cooling rack

Instructions

  1. Stage ingredients & equipment.

    Preheat oven when ready to bake at 350°F. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper.

  2. Make the wet mixture
    1. In the wet bowl, add the softened butter. Beat on low until light and fluffy (~1 minute).
    2. Add brown sugar and granulated sugar. Beat on low until creamed and smooth (3–5 minutes). Scrape sides as needed.
    3. Add eggs one at a time (20 seconds each). Scrape sides as needed.
    4. Add vanilla extract (about 20 seconds mixing).
  3. Prepare the dry mixture
    1. In a separate bowl combine flour, baking soda, corn starch, and kosher salt.
    2. Whisk dry ingredients well to combine.
  4. Combine wet & dry
    1. Slowly add the dry mixture to the wet bowl while mixing on low. Mix until just combined — do not overmix.
    2. Fold in the chocolate chips with a spatula until evenly distributed.
  5. Portion & chill (options)
    1. Scoop dough (3 tbsp per cookie) onto parchment-lined cookie sheets. Six scoops per sheet is suggested.
    2. Chill trays for at least 10 minutes before baking. You may chill overnight; remove from fridge ~10 minutes before baking.
  6. Bake
    1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Bake 12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
    3. After baking, transfer cookies (on parchment) to cooling rack. Let cool completely before storing.
  7. Repeat Repeat scooping/baking steps for remaining dough.

Notes

Use parchment to easily remove cookies from sheet and for even browning. Store in an airtight container. Can use smaller scoop, just decrease cook time by 2 minutes.
